# EcoTrack Platform

EcoTrack was designed to help companies monitor and reduce their carbon footprint in real-time. The platform integrates with various IoT sensors and enterprise APIs to gather data, process it, and display it on an intuitive dashboard.

## The Challenge

The primary challenge was handling large streams of time-series data while maintaining a responsive UI. We needed a database solution capable of fast inserts and complex aggregations.

## The Solution

We utilized TimescaleDB (built on PostgreSQL) for the backend storage and implemented a robust WebSocket architecture for live updates. On the frontend, React paired with customized charting libraries ensured the data was both beautiful and actionable.

### Key Features

1.  **Real-time Dashboard:** Live carbon emissions tracking.
2.  **Automated Reporting:** Generate compliance reports with a single click.
3.  **IoT Integration:** Seamlessly connect to existing factory sensors.

## Outcome

EcoTrack helped pilot companies reduce their carbon footprint by an average of 12% within the first six months of deployment by providing actionable insights into high-emission activities.
